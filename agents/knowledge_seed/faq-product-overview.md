# What Meridian Mechanical does

Meridian Mechanical Group is a commercial HVAC and plumbing services firm serving mid-market
properties across central Texas. We do four things for our customers:

- **Preventive maintenance agreements.** Quarterly scheduled service on a customer's rooftop
  units, chillers, boilers, and walk-in refrigeration, with filters, belts, and coil service
  included. This is the recurring-revenue core of the business.
- **Emergency and on-demand repair.** Priority dispatch for failures — a tripped chiller, a
  failed evaporator-fan motor, a dock-door heater out in winter. Service-agreement customers get
  a contractual response-time commitment.
- **Installation and retrofit.** Replacing aging equipment: rooftop-unit swaps, chiller
  retrofits to high-efficiency magnetic-bearing units, boiler retrofits, and building-automation
  (BAS) upgrades.
- **Compliance and commissioning support.** Test-and-balance, IQ/OQ commissioning
  documentation, and air-balance reports for regulated facilities (healthcare, food service).

Our ideal customer is a facilities or operations leader at a property-management firm, a
school district, a healthcare or industrial site, or a hospitality group — someone responsible
for uptime who would rather have one accountable vendor than chase trades. We compete on
reliability, documentation, and lifecycle cost, not on the lowest sticker price.
