# Service-agreement rate card

Agreement pricing is built from unit count, site count, and response tier. Use this as a
starting frame, then adjust for equipment age and access difficulty.

**Base annual ranges**

- Single small site (under 15 units): $18,000–$30,000/yr
- Mid site or 2–3 sites (15–40 units): $30,000–$54,000/yr
- Large or multi-site (40+ units): $54,000–$72,000/yr

**Response-tier modifiers**

- Standard (4-hour business-hours): included in base
- Priority (2-hour): +10–15%
- Critical (same-hour, named facility): +20–30%

**Structured discounts (inside the 10% floor)**

- Multi-site (3+ locations on one agreement): up to 7%
- Multi-year (2–3 year term): up to 8%
- These stack only with VP sign-off; combined they can exceed the 10% AE limit.

**Value-adds that defend price without discounting**

- No-charge year-one filter program
- Semi-annual coil cleaning included as a loyalty inclusion on renewal
- Documented deficiency report after every visit (capital-planning data the customer can use)

Always quote the response tier explicitly. A competitor's lower number almost always excludes
after-hours response and filter media — the rate card exists so you can show the customer
exactly what they would be giving up.
