# Reference: equipment glossary

A quick reference for the equipment families Meridian services and the product lines we install,
so you can speak credibly on a walkthrough. (All product names are Meridian's standard lines.)

**Chillers** — central cooling for large buildings. The **NorthCool C-450** is a centrifugal
chiller common in older central plants; aging C-450s on R-22 refrigerant are prime retrofit
candidates. Modern retrofits use high-efficiency **magnetic-bearing** chillers — quieter, far
more efficient, and no oil management.

**Rooftop units (RTUs)** — packaged heating/cooling units on a building's roof. The **Kestrel
KX-90** is a workhorse commercial RTU; fleets of aging KX-90s drive both PM agreements and
replacement programs.

**Boilers** — the **TruFlow TF-8** is our standard commercial boiler line for heating and
domestic hot water.

**Air-handling units (AHUs)** — move and condition air; critical in healthcare for HEPA
filtration and room-pressure control.

**Makeup-air and ventilation** — the **AeroVent AV-2** makeup-air unit balances exhaust in
kitchens and labs.

**Cooling towers** — the **ClimaCore CC-30** rejects heat from chiller plants; fill replacement
is a common scoped option on renewals.

**Walk-in refrigeration** — the **Permafrost PF-9** walk-in unit; failed evaporator-fan motors
and worn door gaskets are the usual service calls in food service.

**R-22** — a legacy refrigerant being phased out; its cost and scarcity make major repairs on
R-22 equipment throwaway money and a strong retrofit argument.
