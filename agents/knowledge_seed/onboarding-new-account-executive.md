# Onboarding: your first two weeks

Welcome to Meridian Mechanical. Your job is to help facilities and operations leaders keep
their buildings running, and to do it with a vendor relationship they trust. Here's how to get
productive fast.

**Week one — learn the product and the proof.** Read the services overview, the service-
agreement FAQ, and the pricing policy. Sit in on two QBRs and one site walkthrough. Learn the
uptime and response-time numbers cold — they are your most persuasive assets.

**Week one — learn the system.** Every account, deal, contact, and activity lives in the CRM.
The pipeline stages are new → qualified → proposal → negotiation → closed_won / closed_lost.
Log every customer interaction as an activity; the agent and your manager both rely on a clean,
current record. See the CRM-hygiene guide.

**Week two — learn the guardrails.** You can discount up to 10% at the table; above that needs
VP sign-off and the system will hold it for approval. Side-effecting actions — sending a
customer email, moving a deal, issuing a quote — route through the Greenlight approval queue
unless your account's autonomy level auto-approves them. Read the Greenlight onboarding doc so
the queue isn't a surprise.

**Week two — take a live deal.** Shadow a renewal and a competitive situation. Practice the
discovery and renewal playbooks. Your manager will hand you a small repair or upsell to close
end-to-end.

Ask questions early. The fastest-ramping AEs are the ones who over-communicate in month one.
